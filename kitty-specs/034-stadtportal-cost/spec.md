# Feature: Stadtportal Cost

## Problem
The town portal (Stadtportal) is currently free to use, making it a no-risk escape. It should have a cost to add a resource management decision.

## Goal
Add a gold cost to the town portal. The portal scroll should be purchasable from Mara in the potion tab.

## Functional Requirements
- **FR-001**: Town portal is no longer free — requires a portal scroll item
- **FR-002**: Portal scrolls are sold by Mara in the potion tab
- **FR-003**: Portal scroll has a gold cost that scales or is fixed (balance TBD)
- **FR-004**: Player cannot use town portal without a scroll in inventory
- **FR-005**: UI shows remaining portal scrolls and indicates when none are available
